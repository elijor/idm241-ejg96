Triggers

  • User hovers on product image tile
  • User hovers off of product image tile
  • User hovers on favorite heart
  • User hovers off favorite heart

Rules

User hovers on product image tile

  • Cursor change from default arrow to pointer
  • Initial product image slides to the left while another image slides in from right to take its place in .5 seconds
  • Quick shop bar overlay slides from onscreen left to visible right in .4 seconds
  • Favorite heart scales horizontally from 0 to 100% centrally
  • Product title quickly fades in .3 seconds from a black color to blue gray font color

User hovers off of product image tile

  • Cursor change from pointer to default arrow
  • Second product image slides to the right while inital image slides from left to take its place in .5 seconds
  • Quick shop bar overlay slides from right to left in .4 seconds and is not longer visible
  • Favorite heart scales horizontally from 100% to 0 centrally and is no longer visible
  • Product title quickly fades in .3 seconds from a blue gray color back to black font color

User hovers on favorite heart

  • Heart icon's gray fill fades to black fill in .3 seconds

User hovers off favorite heart

  • Heart icon's black fill fades to gray fill in .3 seconds
